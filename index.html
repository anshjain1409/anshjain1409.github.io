Uber Revenue Optimization Analysis

Increased revenue understanding on 10M+ trips, leading to 5 strategic recommendations.

Situation: Uber needed to understand the key factors influencing profitability across more than 10 million trips, but lacked clear insights from their raw trip data.

Task: My goal was to analyze this large-scale dataset to identify the primary drivers of "Revenue per Trip" and provide actionable recommendations to improve profitability.

Action: I used Python and SQL to process, clean, and query the 10M+ trip records. I then built interactive Tableau dashboards to visualize the relationship between revenue and variables like trip type and payment method.

Result: The analysis successfully identified that airport trips and credit card payments were key drivers, generating over 40% higher revenue. This led to 5 strategic recommendations for optimizing fare structures and payment promotions.

Tools Used: Python, SQL, Tableau Analyzed 10M+ trip records using Python and SQL; visualized trends via interactive Tableau dashboards.

Flipkart Customer Service Optimization

Performed RCA on 100K+ service tickets, projecting to raise CSAT by 15% and reduce complaints by 25%.

Situation: Flipkart's customer service department was experiencing a high volume of complaints, but the team lacked insight into the systemic root causes, leading to repeat issues and lower CSAT scores.

Task: My goal was to conduct a comprehensive Root Cause Analysis (RCA) on 100,000+ service tickets to identify and cluster recurring problems.

Action: I used SQL and advanced Excel functions to query, filter, and segment the 100K+ tickets. I grouped them by issue type and product category to pinpoint the most frequent and severe systemic problems.

Result: The RCA uncovered several key operational gaps. I formulated optimization recommendations that were projected to raise CSAT by 15% and reduce overall complaint volume by 25%.

Tools Used: Excel, SQL Advanced Excel functions and SQL queries were used to segment and analyze over 100,000 customer service tickets for root cause analysis.

Sentiment Analysis of Economic Conditions

Built a pipeline to process 1,500+ financial headlines, providing early sentiment signals.

Situation: Economic forecasting was hindered by a lack of real-time, quantified market insight, forcing analysts to manually interpret thousands of financial news headlines.

Task: I set out to engineer an end-to-end NLP pipeline to automate the collection and sentiment analysis of financial news, providing early, quantified economic signals.
