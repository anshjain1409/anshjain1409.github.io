Uber Revenue Optimization Analysis

Increased revenue understanding on 10M+ trips, leading to 5 strategic recommendations.

Situation: Uber needed to understand the key factors influencing profitability across more than 10 million trips, but lacked clear insights from their raw trip data.

Task: My goal was to analyze this large-scale dataset to identify the primary drivers of "Revenue per Trip" and provide actionable recommendations to improve profitability.

Action: I used Python and SQL to process, clean, and query the 10M+ trip records. I then built interactive Tableau dashboards to visualize the relationship between revenue and variables like trip type and payment method.

Result: The analysis successfully identified that airport trips and credit card payments were key drivers, generating over 40% higher revenue. This led to 5 strategic recommendations for optimizing fare structures and payment promotions.

Tools Used: Python, SQL, Tableau Analyzed 10M+ trip records using Python and SQL; visualized trends via interactive Tableau dashboards.
